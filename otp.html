      Contract of Sale | OTP Generator

      Welcome tot the Brune Attorneys Contract of Sale App. This App automates the process of drafting a legally binding contract of sale.

      The information required is divided into six sections:

      • Seller
      • Purchaser
      • Property
      • Price
      • General
      • Agent & Conveyancer

      Navigate freely between the sections and complete the fields (required fields are indicated by the red asterisk).

      Click on any of the information icons to see more detailed information about that field.

      Click 'Submit' once a section is complete to validate that the entered information is correct. Successfully validated sections will be indicated with a green tick.

      Once all six sections have been successfully validated the 'Generate' section will became accessible. Simply enter your email address in the 'Generate' section and your Contract of Sale will be drafted and sent to your inbox.
